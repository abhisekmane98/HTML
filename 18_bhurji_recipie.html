Bhurji

Bhurji is healthy breakfast. provide you enough amount of prot iens and energy.

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 onions
  • 1/2 tomato
  • fenugreek
  • half salt
  • 1 1/2 spoon chilli

procedure

  1. pour 4-5 spoon oil in your karhai and boil them
  2. pour cutted tomato, onion in that and fry well for 7-8 minutes
  3. Then break the eggs and pour into this and then fry 7-8 minutes on medium gas
  4. Then spread fenugreek on top of that
  5. your dish is ready
